Field notes: Earth

  • Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
  • Earth is the fifth-largest planet in our solar system.
  • Earth has an average radius of about 6,371 kilometers.
  • Earth has one moon and no rings.
  • Earth is the only planet currently known to support life.
  • Earth is the only planet in our solar system with liquid water on its surface.
  • About 71% of Earth’s surface is covered by ocean.
