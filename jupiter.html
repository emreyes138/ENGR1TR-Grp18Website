Field notes: Jupiter

  • Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system.
  • Jupiter has an equatorial radius of about 71,492 kilometers.
  • One day on Jupiter lasts only about 9.9 hours, the shortest day of any planet.
  • Jupiter takes about 12 Earth years to orbit the Sun.
  • Jupiter is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium.
  • Jupiter does not have a true solid surface.
  • Jupiter has faint rings and many moons, including Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
  • Jupiter’s Great Red Spot is a giant storm that has lasted for centuries.
