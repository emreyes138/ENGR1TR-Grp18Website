Field notes: Mars

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun.
  • Mars is about half as wide as Earth.
  • Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, and no rings.
  • The Martian atmosphere is thin and is made mostly of carbon dioxide.
  • Mars is called the Red Planet because iron minerals in its soil oxidize, or rust.
  • Evidence shows that ancient Mars had rivers, lakes, and a thicker atmosphere.
  • Olympus Mons on Mars is the largest volcano in the solar system.
