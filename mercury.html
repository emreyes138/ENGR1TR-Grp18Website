Field notes: Mercury

  • Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
  • Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system.
  • Daytime temperatures on Mercury can reach about 800°F (430°C).
  • Mercury is a rocky planet with a heavily cratered surface.
  • Mercury has no moons and no rings.
  • Mercury has a very thin exosphere instead of a thick atmosphere.
  • Mercury completes one orbit around the Sun in only 88 Earth days.
