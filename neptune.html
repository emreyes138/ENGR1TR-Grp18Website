Field notes: Neptune

  • Neptune is the eighth and most distant planet from the Sun.
  • Neptune is the fourth-largest planet and is about four times wider than Earth.
  • Neptune is an ice giant made of materials including water, methane, and ammonia.
  • Neptune does not have a solid surface.
  • One day on Neptune lasts about 16 hours.
  • Neptune takes about 165 Earth years to orbit the Sun.
  • Neptune has 16 known moons, and Triton is the largest.
  • Neptune has at least five main rings and four prominent ring arcs.
  • Neptune is the windiest planet, with winds faster than 1,200 miles per hour.
