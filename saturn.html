Field notes: Saturn

  • Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun.
  • Saturn is the second-largest planet in our solar system.
  • Saturn is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium.
  • Saturn does not have a true solid surface.
  • One day on Saturn lasts about 10.7 hours.
  • Saturn takes about 29.4 Earth years to orbit the Sun.
  • Saturn’s rings are made of billions of pieces of ice and rock.
  • Saturn has more known moons than any other planet.
  • Titan and Enceladus are two of Saturn’s best-known moons.
