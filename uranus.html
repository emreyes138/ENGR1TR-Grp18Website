Field notes: Uranus

  • Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun.
  • Uranus is the third-largest planet and is about four times wider than Earth.
  • Uranus is an ice giant containing water, methane, and ammonia deep inside.
  • Uranus does not have a true solid surface.
  • One day on Uranus lasts about 17 hours.
  • Uranus takes about 84 Earth years to orbit the Sun.
  • Uranus is tilted almost 90 degrees, making it appear to spin on its side.
  • Methane in the atmosphere gives Uranus its blue-green color.
  • Uranus has 13 faint rings and 28 known moons.
