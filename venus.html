Field notes: Venus

  • Venus is the second planet from the Sun.
  • Venus is similar in size and structure to Earth, so it is sometimes called Earth’s twin.
  • Venus rotates in the opposite direction from most planets.
  • One rotation on Venus takes about 243 Earth days.
  • Venus has no moons and no rings.
  • Venus has a thick atmosphere made mostly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid.
  • A runaway greenhouse effect makes Venus the hottest planet in our solar system.
  • The average surface temperature on Venus is about 872°F (467°C).
